Intro

Hi, I'm Koena. As a data analyst I turn raw data into meaningful insights and interpret the results of my analysis to key stakeholders, guiding them in making data-driven decisions.

Work

CLEANING DATA IN SQL - In this project I clean housing data using sql, from removing duplicates to standardizing the data. I also alter tables and populate empty rows.

Data Exploration with SQL - I explore a Covid-19 dataset to find interesting trends and patterns. As part of my exploratory data analysis, I create views and temp tables to visualize in tableau to discover trends that may have otherwise been hidden.

Analysis and Visualizations with Python - In this project find correlations between attributes in a movie dataset. I use scatter plots and heat maps to visualize the correlations.

About

I am a (Big) Data Professional who has a strong passion for generating meaningful and actionable insights. I am proficient in creating Dashboards, building Reports, Data Cleaning, Database Management, and creating performance-related insights. Automating processes concerning Data Extraction and Data Transformation also falls under my area of expertise. Through extensive exploratory data analysis (EDA), I can find trends within datasets and thereafter translate them into visualizations and operational dashboards. My goal is to always communicate my findings clearly to stakeholders/audiences despite their level of knowledge in data analytics.
